# 快速录入模板

复制以下模板，填入从截图中提取的文字。

---

## 模板

```markdown
### D{编号} - {题目主题} ({来源})
**Category:** {Education/Society/Technology/Environment/Economy/Culture/Other}  
**Source:** {OG/Real/Practice}  
**Image:** `images/d{编号}-{简短名称}.png`

**Professor Question:**
{粘贴教授问题}

**Student Posts:**
- **{作者1}:** {粘贴第一个学生回复}
- **{作者2}:** {粘贴第二个学生回复}

**Notes:** 
- {可选备注}

**Status:** ⏳
```

---

## 示例

```markdown
### D07 - Automation (Practice)
**Category:** Technology  
**Source:** Practice  
**Image:** `images/d07-automation.png`

**Professor Question:**
Do you think that automation and artificial intelligence will replace most human jobs in the future? Why or why not?

**Student Posts:**
- **Alex:** I think automation will replace many jobs, especially repetitive ones. But it will also create new types of jobs that we can't imagine yet. People will need to learn new skills to work with AI instead of being replaced by it.
- **Sam:** I'm worried about job loss. Many people work in jobs that could be automated, and they might not have the education or resources to learn new skills. Governments and companies need to help workers transition to new careers.

**Notes:** 
- 题目完整，2个学生回复
- 主题：自动化对就业的影响

**Status:** ⏳
```

## 分类参考

- **Education**: 教育、学习、学校相关
- **Society**: 社会、人际关系、社区
- **Technology**: 科技、AI、数字化
- **Environment**: 环境、气候、可持续发展
- **Economy**: 经济、商业、金融
- **Culture**: 文化、艺术、传统
- **Other**: 其他

---

## 来源标记

- **OG**: Official Guide 官方指南题目
- **Real**: 真题
- **Practice**: 模拟题/练习题
